## Step 2

If the game gets wild, our sprite might leave the screen.  
Let's prevent that. 

---

ðŸ”² From the ``||sprites:Sprites||`` category, grab a 
``||scene:set [mySprite] [auto destroy] <OFF>||`` 
block and drag it into the end of the **on start** container. 

ðŸ”² On the new block, click **auto destroy** to open a dropdown
menu full of choices and choose **stay in screen**.

ðŸ”² Toggle **`<OFF>`** to **`<ON>`** to keep your sprite inside the
play area at all times.


