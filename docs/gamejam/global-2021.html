Global MakeCode Arcade Game Jam!

Microsoft MakeCode has partnered with US State Department to bring you the inaugural  Global MakeCode Arcade Game Jam!  Practice your game development skills in this worldwide competition using MakeCode Arcade, a platform that lets you build your own retro-style video game.

Once the theme of the Game Jam is announced on November 29th, you'll have three weeks to create and submit your game. We can't wait to see what you make!

Theme Announcement

The countdown starts now! Tune-in on November 29th at 9 AM PST to find out the theme of the game jam and find out what you need to do to get started.

Submit Your Game

Don’t to forget to submit your game before December 17th for your chance to be featured! Standout games will be highlighted here and on MakeCode social media channels.

Follow us on Instagram, Facebook or Twitter to find out who made our list of favorites.
